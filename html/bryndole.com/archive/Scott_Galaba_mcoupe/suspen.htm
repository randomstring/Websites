The current Dinan Strut Bar does not fit the '99 coupes so how does one modify the bar to fit?

On the passenger side suspension strut dome is a special nut that BMW installed for jump starting the car. The nut is now mounted a little differently than the '98s and the Dinan strut bar comes in contact with it preventing the install of their bar. One can remove a little material (with a Dremel tool, etc.) off the Dinan bar to get the bar to fit around the nut.
