Privacy Policy

Bottlecount.com respects and protects the privacy of the individuals that use Bottlecount.com's services ("Bottlecount.com Services"). Individually identifiable information about you is not willfully disclosed to any third party without first receiving your permission, as explained in this privacy policy ("Privacy Policy").

Bottlecount.com and Cookies

Upon your first visit to Bottlecount.com, Bottlecount.com sends a "cookie" to your computer. A cookie is a piece of data that identifies you as a unique user. Bottlecount.com uses cookies to grant access to authenticated (logged in) users. Bottlecount.com will not disclose its cookies to third parties except as required by a valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.

Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. Be aware, however, that some parts of the Bottlecount.com Services may not function properly if you refuse cookies.

What Information Do We Collect?

Bottlecount.com does not collect any unique information about you (such as your name, email address, etc.) except when you specifically and knowingly provide such information. Bottlecount.com notes and saves information such as time of day, browser type, browser language, and IP address with each query. That information is used to verify our records and to provide more relevant services to users. For example, Bottlecount.com may use your IP address or browser language to determine which language to use when showing search results or advertisements.

Links to Other Sites

The stories/sites displayed and/or linked to by the Bottlecount.com Service are developed by people over whom Bottlecount.com exercises no control. Other links are also on sites not controlled by Bottlecount.com. These other sites may send their own cookies to users, collect data, or solicit personal information. Bottlecount.com may choose to exhibit its results in the form of a "URL redirecter." When Bottlecount.com uses a URL redirecter, if you click on a URL on a Bottlecount.com page, information about the click is sent to Bottlecount.com, and Bottlecount.com in turn sends you to the site you clicked on. Bottlecount.com uses this URL information to understand and improve the quality of Bottlecount.com's technology.

With Whom Does Bottlecount.com Share Information?

Bottlecount.com may share information about you with advertisers, business partners, sponsors, and other third parties. However, we only divulge aggregate information about our users and will not share personally identifiable information with any third party without your express consent. For example, we may disclose how frequently the average Bottlecount.com user visits Bottlecount.com, or which Bottlecount.com pages are most often used. Please be aware, however, that we will release specific personal information about you if required to do so in order to comply with any valid legal process such as a search warrant, subpoena, statute, or court order.

Your consent and changes to the Privacy Policy

By using the Bottlecount.com Services and our web site, you consent to the collection and use of your information as we have outlined in this policy and to our Terms of Service Policy.
