SSR Competition Wheels on an M coupe

The following pictures show SSR Competition Wheels on an M coupe. The front wheels are 17x8" in 40mm offset and the rears are 17x9.5" in 15mm offset. What's nice about this setup is the offset of the SSR rear wheel being a 15mm compared to a 8mm from the stock M coupe wheels and it's so hard to find wheels that fit the M coupe with out spending a lot for custom wheels.

In the pictures below you will notice that wheels studs have been installed on all four hubs and a H&R spacer in the front of 15mm as standard use when using the Big Red Brakes up front. The brake upgrade in the rear adds 4mm to the track of both sides of the car when using a stock wheel but in this case it brings 15mm offset down to an 11mm offset. The SSR Competition Wheels weight savings over stock is a great improvement; 14-15lbs a wheel compared to 21lbs & 24lbs.
