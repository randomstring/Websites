Fire Bottle Halon Fire Suppression System

Below are pictures showing the installation of a Fire Bottle Halon Fire Suppression System. Shown here is the 5.5 lbs electrical activated system.

Item Included with System:

  • Fire Bottle with gauge that indicates charge level
  • Power Pack
  • Wiring Assembly
  • Push Button
  • 8' Alloy Delivery Tube
  • 2 Nozzles
  • Brass Tee Adapter
  • Brass Discharge Adapter
  • Mounting Bracket with Clamps

BMW Parts Needed:

  • 51 16 2 492 136 - blanks for center console
