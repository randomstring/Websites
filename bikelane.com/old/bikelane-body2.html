A word from your host

    When I created this page in 1993, there weren't more than about 6 noteworthy bicycling related Web pages. Now there are too many to keep track. I received many additions and corrections every week, so its a real challenge to keep on top of things. I have since graduated from Purdue university, and now work for Sun Microsystems. Work doesn't always give me the time I need to keep maintain this page the way I'd like, but I can now afford my own domain www.Bikelane.com so now it will be that much easier to remember the URL.

    As always, please continue to help me out by mailing me new links. I'm looking to keep all the sections fresh with new additions. If you do send me a link please send it in the following format: 

    "http://www.address.com/path" "link text" "short description"
    This format makes my life much easier, and allows me to keep the WWW Bikelane up to date.

    Thanks,
