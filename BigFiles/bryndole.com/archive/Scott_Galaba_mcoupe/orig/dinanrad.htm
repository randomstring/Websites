Dinan Custom Aluminium Radiator

Below are pictures showing the Dinan Custom Aluminium Radiator designed to be used on BMW's that need a little extra cooling. Dimension of the custom radiator are 21" x 23" x 2-1/4" (h x w x d). In the photos below note how the radiator has all the tabs and connection points just like the stock radiator. Also notice that the radiator has aluminium tanks instead of the black plastic used of a stock one.
