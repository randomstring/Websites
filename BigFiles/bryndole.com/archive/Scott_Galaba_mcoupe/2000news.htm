BMW M coupe
2000 Model Information

The following are the changes to the M coupe:

  • Chrome trim on front grille slats
  • Chrome ring around headlights
  • Titanium Silver paint replaces Arctic Silver
  • Oxford Green paint replaces Boston Green

All other Options and Features are the same as for the 1999 model.
