What will the roof rack system consist of for the coupes?

At the Chicago Auto show on a 328i, BMW had the roof rack system that will be on our coupes from what I could tell. On the roof of your coupe you will find 4 small little doors that lift up, look for the black trim on the roof. Lift up and towards the middle of the car to open doors. Inside you will find a nut that has been welded to the roof frame. The roof rack system is made up of two bars that have two upright supports one on either end. The supports attach via a bolt to the nut on the roof frame. You can then attach to the bar addition pieces of the system to mount skies, bikes, etc.
